Empowering Rural Governance through Digitalization

A comprehensive, digital platform designed to improve transparency, efficiency, and communication in Gram Panchayat administration.

The Challenge & Solution

The Challenge

Lack of centralized data, slow grievance redressal, and low transparency in fund utilization often hamper village development and citizen trust.

Our Solution (Village 360)

A unified dashboard providing real-time data access for officials and a user-friendly interface for citizens to track projects and register complaints.
